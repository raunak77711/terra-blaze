Sarah O'Connell

Verified Trekker

Dublin, Ireland

★★★★★
Chitwan Safari

Added Chitwan onto the end of our Nepal trip almost as an afterthought and it turned out to be a highlight. Canoeing past a resting gharial at sunrise, then a jeep safari that got us within maybe 40 metres of a one-horned rhino — genuinely couldn't believe it. The Tharu cultural dance in the evening was a lovely way to close out the day.

Everest Base Camp

Fourteen days, one bad night in Dingboche with a pounding headache, and the single best sunrise of my life from Kala Patthar. This is exactly how EBC should be done.

Manaslu Circuit

Manaslu is quieter than the big-name treks and that was exactly what I wanted. Crossing Larkya La before sunrise, in the cold and the dark, then watching the light hit the pass from the other side — I will remember that morning for the rest of my life. Logistics for the restricted area permit were handled without a single hiccup on my end.
